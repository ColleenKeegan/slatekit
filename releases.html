Slate Kit

Releases

All the recent and upcoming releases of Slate Kit

We are very happy to announce that SlateKit is now officially publicly available as a Beta Release. We have been using SlateKit internally on projects for over 1.5 years with much success. The investment in Scala, Akka and Akka-Http has paid off tremendously, by providing a modern, concise, cross-platform language with great tools and frameworks to build high-performance, scalable mobile and web applications. We are offering much of the SlateKit as open-source while some parts are paid/premium.

SlateKit is now publicly available as an beta-release.

As the first beta release, SlateKit may have bugs/issues.
Please feel free to contact us at www.github.com\codehelix or at www.github.com\kishorereddy
